Study Design

Our crowdsourced study follows a between-subjective design, we recruited 180 participants from AmazonMT.

Study Conditions

We created four study conditions to study the effect of linking interaction and layout settings on visual story reading experience:

  • Original Vertical
  • Original Vertical with Linking
  • Slideshow
  • Slideshow with Linking

Data Collection

We collected data including time spent, interaction performed, comprehension/recall/engagemention tasks answers, subjective metrics.

Statistical Result in Paper

We listed all statistical model results for linking here:
